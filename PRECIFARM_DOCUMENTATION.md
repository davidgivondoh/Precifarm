## 1. Company Overview

**Precifarm** is a Kenyan agri-engineering company that designs, supplies, installs, and maintains precision agricultural water and energy infrastructure. We serve smallholder farmers, commercial agribusinesses, institutions, and large-scale estate developers across all 38+ counties in Kenya.

### Mission
Engineer agricultural systems that perform from day one and pay for themselves within months — with guaranteed uptime and absolute ROI calculated before breaking ground.

### What We Do (Summary)
- **Solar-powered water pumping** for boreholes, rivers, and open water sources
- **Precision drip irrigation** from quarter-acre kits to 10+ acre commercial systems
- **Turnkey farm design** including topographic surveys, hydraulic engineering, and full project management
- **Solar energy systems** (residential, commercial, and farm) under the **Nishati** brand

Precifarm provides principal engineering consulting for medium and large-scale agricultural development. Services integrate water sourcing, pumping, storage, distribution, precision irrigation, and energy systems into a single engineered solution optimized for ROI.

#### Design Process

| Step | Activities | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| **01 Discovery** | Site visit, stakeholder needs, water assessment, soil analysis, crop planning | 2–3 weeks |
| **02 Design** | Engineering calculations, hydraulics, specifications, BoQ, cost estimate | 3–6 weeks |
| **03 Implementation** | Procurement, installation, quality control, supervision, testing, commissioning | 4–16 weeks |

#### Service Tiers

---

**Tier 1 — Irrigation Design**
Scale: 5–20 hectares
For targeted irrigation design without full energy integration

Scope of Work:
- Topographic survey of farm
- Water source capacity, quality, and reliability assessment
- Complete pipe sizing and hydraulic pressure calculations
- Block planning, zone allocation, and emitter mapping
- Pump, filter, pipe, and emitter specifications
- Detailed bill of quantities with current pricing

Deliverables:
- AutoCAD/GIS farm layout with dimensions
- Hydraulic calculations (flow rates, pressures, losses)
- Equipment specifications (exact models and quantities)
- Itemized Bill of Quantities
- Installation drawings for contractors
- Operation and maintenance manual
